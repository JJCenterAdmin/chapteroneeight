Who We Are

Mission & Vision.

To be found and known in Christ (Philippians 3:9) so that we can live out God's will for us to prosper and have good success (Joshua 1:8).

Mission

Rooted in His Word.

The Book of the Law shall not depart out of our mouth, but we shall meditate on it day and night, that we may observe and do according to all that is written in it. For then God shall make our ways prosperous, and then we shall deal wisely and have good success.

Vision

A Brunch & Book Club.

A monthly gathering where people join in laughter, motivation, support, prayer, and good food. We come together to discuss the books we have read, the scriptures we have been meditating on, and to testify about how God is helping us deal wisely and walk in good success.

Find your path.

ChapterOneEight gathers virtually every Monday and in person every month. Choose the path God is calling you to.
